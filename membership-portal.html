Approval Workflow — How Your Information Goes Live
Seven-step mandatory process. Minimum three human approvals required before any data is published.
1

Member Submits or Updates Information

Family submits registration form or logs in to update existing record. System assigns a reference number and timestamps the submission. Member is shown a confirmation screen.

2

Automatic Acknowledgement

An automated email is sent to the member’s primary email address confirming receipt. The email includes the reference number and a note that the submission is pending approval.

3

Designated Approvers Notified

The church Secretary, President/Vicar, and all Trustees receive an email notification with a link to the Admin Review Queue. Approvers may act in any order.

4

Minimum Three Human Approvals Required

The following combinations are the only valid approval paths:

  • ✓ President/Vicar + any two Trustees
  • ✓ President/Vicar + one Trustee + Secretary
  • ✓ President/Vicar + Secretary + one other authorised ExCo member

The Administrator cannot bypass this rule. The system enforces it automatically.

5

Directory & Portal Updated

Once the third qualifying approval is recorded, the central directory database is updated. The member portal shows the new approved data. The printed directory queue is updated.

6

Changes Requested or Rejected

If any approver clicks Request Changes or Reject, an automated email is sent to the member with the approver’s comments. The submission remains in Pending status until the member resubmits and the approval process restarts.

7

Audit Log Updated

Every event — submission, approval, rejection, comment, and publication — is written to a permanent, tamper-resistant audit log with the approver name, role, and timestamp. Visible to Administrator and Vicar only.
