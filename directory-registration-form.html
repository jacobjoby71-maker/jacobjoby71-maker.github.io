What is the Home Parish (Idavaka)?

Every Mar Thoma family, whether in Kerala or the diaspora, retains formal membership in a Home Parish (Idavaka) in Kerala/India. This is captured in every Mar Thoma directory worldwide including Dubai (marthomaparishdubai.org) and the Diocese of North America (marthomana.org). It is a record of spiritual heritage and family identity.
