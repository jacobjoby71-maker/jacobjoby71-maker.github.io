Executive Summary

The case for the Silver Jubilee Parish Directory

St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Vancouver marks its 25th Anniversary (Silver Jubilee) in 2025. The Directory Committee proposes the creation of a comprehensive, print-ready Parish Membership Directory — a permanent keepsake capturing the church's families, history, and community as it enters its second quarter-century of ministry in Canada.

The directory follows the North American Parish Directory Standard, building on best practices from churches across the Mar Thoma Diocese of North America and drawing on the Immanuel Mar Thoma Church, Paruthippara model for technical production. It will be compiled from a live online registration system accessible via the church website, Facebook, and WhatsApp — making it easy for every family regardless of technical ability.

Project Rationale & Background

Why now, and why this format

Founded in 2000, St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Vancouver has grown from a small gathering of Malayalam-speaking families in British Columbia into a vibrant parish community worshipping at 10787 128 Street, Surrey. The Silver Jubilee marks 25 years of faithful ministry on Canadian soil.

A Parish Directory serves multiple vital functions for the church community: it connects families across prayer groups who may not know one another well; it provides the finance committee with an accurate membership roll; it gives the General Body a formal record of the congregation; and it provides every family with a beautiful, lasting souvenir of their church family at this historic milestone.

The Immanuel Mar Thoma Church, Paruthippara recently demonstrated that a 400-page parish directory can be produced using a data-driven workflow (Google Sheets → MySQL → PHP/FPDF → PDF generation) in approximately 20 seconds of compile time. This proposal adapts that technical model for the North American context, using a cloud-based, mobile-first registration system appropriate for the Vancouver parish.

📋
No Current Membership Roll
The church does not currently have a complete, verified, up-to-date family directory. This project creates that foundational record.
🍁
North American Context
Canadian directories differ from Kerala counterparts: bilingual entries (English/Malayalam), Canadian address format, privacy-compliant data handling (PIPEDA).

North American Directory Standard

Adapted from Mar Thoma Diocese of North America best practices

The following standard has been derived from parish directories produced by Mar Thoma churches across North America, including congregations in New York, Toronto, Chicago, Houston, and the Greater Vancouver Area. It has been adapted to meet Canadian privacy standards and North American typographic conventions.

📐
Print Specifications
Format: 8.5" × 11" (Letter, North American standard)
Binding: Perfect-bound softcover or hardcover
Colour: Full-colour cover; interior mix of colour and B&W
Paper: 100gsm interior; 300gsm cover
Estimated pages: 400–500
Print run: 250 copies (one per registered family + archive)
📝
Entry Standard — Per Family
Family name & house name (if applicable)
Head of family: name, DOB, occupation
Spouse: name, DOB, occupation
Children: names, DOBs, school/grade
Contact: phone, email (optional)
Home address (province/city only for privacy)
Family photo (group portrait)
Prayer group, membership ref. no.
Hometown / place of origin (Kerala)
🍁
Canadian Privacy (PIPEDA) Compliance
All data collected with explicit consent via the registration form. Members may opt out of showing full address, phone, or email. Data stored on Canadian servers or Google Workspace (Canadian data residency available). Retention policy: directory data retained for 7 years in accordance with CRA requirements for charitable organisations. Privacy officer: Church Treasurer.

Digital-First Member Registration System

How families register — zero technical barriers

The registration system has been designed so that any member of the congregation can register their family without technical knowledge. Registration is accessible through four channels, all feeding into a single database:

🌐
Church Website
Accessible at vancouvermarthomachurch.com/directory — permanent, always available, optimal for desktop and laptop users. Full-form experience with photo upload.
📘
Facebook Group Link
Shared in the church Facebook group with a ready-to-post announcement. Rich link preview shows automatically when posted. Ideal for reaching members who primarily use Facebook.
💬
WhatsApp Community
Link shared via WhatsApp with a pre-written Malayalam/English message. "Open in WhatsApp" button auto-fills the message. Reaches members who do not use Facebook.
🖥
Trustee Tablet / Kiosk
For members without smartphones or who prefer in-person registration. Trustees use the kiosk at Sunday Service to register families. Fully offline-capable.

The six-step registration form captures: Head of Family details, Spouse details, Children (unlimited), Family Photo upload, Prayer Group selection, and Listing Type. The entire process takes approximately 5–7 minutes per family.

✝ RESOLUTIONS FOR GENERAL BODY APPROVAL

1.

That the General Body approves the production of a Silver Jubilee Parish Membership Directory of St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Vancouver, to be released at the Silver Jubilee Celebrations in November 2025.

2.

That the General Body authorises the Executive Committee to constitute a Directory Committee of not fewer than five members, including a Chairperson, Sponsorship Coordinator, Photo/Data Coordinator, Technical Coordinator, and Prayer Group Liaisons.

3.

That the General Body approves the fundraising target of CAD $50,000 through family listing fees and directory sponsorships, to fund the Silver Jubilee Celebrations, with a minimum reserve of CAD $5,000 retained for printing costs.

4.

That the General Body approves the sponsorship rate schedule as presented in Section 5 of this proposal, including the listing fees ranging from CAD $40 (Standard) to CAD $200 (Platinum) per family.

5.

That the General Body authorises the Directory Committee to collect, store, and process family data (including photographs) in accordance with PIPEDA, for the sole purpose of producing the Parish Directory and maintaining the church membership roll.
