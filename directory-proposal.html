1. Overview
Background and purpose of the Silver Jubilee Parish Directory

St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Vancouver was founded in the year 2000, under the pastoral care of the Diocese of North America of the Mar Thoma Syrian Church. In 2025, the congregation celebrates its Silver Jubilee — a milestone of 25 years of worship, fellowship, and Christian service in British Columbia.

To mark this occasion, the Executive Committee proposes the production of a Silver Jubilee Parish Directory 2025: a comprehensive printed and digital record of all member families, church authorities, prayer groups, and the history of the congregation. The directory will be produced in the 8.5" × 11" Letter format standard used across the Diocese of North America.

Alongside the printed directory, the committee proposes the development of a Live Membership Portal — a secure online platform for family registration, profile updates, and directory management, with a mandatory three-approver workflow before any data is published.

This proposal seeks the approval of the Executive Committee and, subsequently, ratification by the General Body (Edavaka Sangham).

⛪
2. Diocesan Alignment — Diocese of North America
Alignment with marthomana.org guidelines and best practices of Mar Thoma parishes in North America and Dubai

This project is explicitly aligned with the guidelines of the Diocese of North America of the Mar Thoma Church (marthomana.org), headquartered at Sinai Mar Thoma Centre, Merrick, New York.

The Family Directory Information Form and the Live Membership Portal are modelled on:

  • The directory standard of the Diocese of North America as observed across its parishes
  • The live membership portal of Mar Thoma Parish Dubai (marthomaparishdubai.org) — serving 2,400+ families — which captures blood group, home parish (Idavaka), all family members’ details, and emergency contacts
  • The Mar Thoma Church’s downloadable family and clergy welfare fund registration forms (marthoma.in)

A copy of this proposal and the finalised directory will be submitted to the Diocesan Bishop, Rt. Rev. Dr. Abraham Mar Paulos Episcopa, for information.

4. Live Membership Portal — Design & Three-Approver Workflow
Secure online platform for family registration and directory management

4.1 Portal Overview

The Live Membership Portal is a secure web-based platform accessible at vancouvermarthomachurch.com. It provides four pages: a Public Information Page, a Secure Login Page, a Member Profile Page, and an Admin Review & Approval Queue.

4.2 Mandatory Three-Approver Workflow

No new or updated family data will be published without the following minimum approvals. The system enforces this rule automatically and it cannot be overridden by any single user, including the Administrator.

Acceptable approval combinations:

  • President / Vicar + any two Trustees
  • President / Vicar + one Trustee + Secretary
  • President / Vicar + Secretary + one other authorised ExCo member

4.3 Workflow Steps

1

Member submits or updates information online

System assigns a reference number. Member sees a confirmation screen immediately.

2

Automated acknowledgement email sent to member

Email confirms receipt and reference number. Data is marked Pending Approval.

3

Approvers notified and review in queue

President/Vicar, Secretary, and all Trustees receive email notification with link to Admin Queue.

4

Three qualifying approvals recorded

Only after the required combination of three approvals is the data released for publication.

5

Directory database and member portal updated

Approved data is immediately reflected in the live online directory and queued for the printed edition.

6

Rejection or change requests returned to member

Automated email with approver comments. Item stays Pending until resubmitted and re-approved.

7

Permanent audit log maintained

Who submitted, who approved/rejected, and date/time — logged for every event.

4.4 Role-Based Access

  • Member: View and edit own record only. Cannot publish.
  • Vicar / President: Key approver. One of the mandatory three. Can view all records.
  • Secretary: Can approve. Can view all records. Cannot bypass three-approver rule.
  • Trustee: Can approve. Can view all records.
  • Directory Convenor: Can review and recommend. Does NOT count toward three-approver rule.
  • Administrator: Full system access. Cannot bypass three-approver rule under any circumstance.

4.5 Privacy

Sensitive contact information (full address, phone, email) is visible only to logged-in members and the church office. The public-facing directory displays only the fields the member approved in their registration form. The portal is designed for future extension to contributions and event registrations.
